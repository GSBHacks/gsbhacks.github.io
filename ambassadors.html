F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should I join?

    Through the GSBHacks Ambassador Program, get ready to gain social media marketing and leadership experience for resume, show initiative on college applications, network with like-minded individuals, and receive priority consideration for future GSBHacks internships and team member positions.

  • How long is the program?

    The GSBHacks Ambassador Program is only two weeks long, the perfect activity that will fit into even the busiest student's schedule.

  • Is there any experience needed?

    No previous hackathon or coding experience required!

  • Who can join?

    People who identify as female or non-binary who are currently attending middle school, high school, and college are eligible.
